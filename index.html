About

CTY4GAMES

Catch Them Young for Games (CTY4GAMES) is a learning program created as a career pathway into the gaming industry to acquire life & tech skills, mentorship for employment, entrepreneurship and prosperity.

With our robust and specially designed curriculum that aims to promote accelerated learning and targeted to meet every aspect of the different segments of the gaming industry, the CTY4Games program will train its students on game production, game play techniques, game development, audio in games, storytelling, publishing, distribution & marketing, art & animation, esports and kickstarting a career in the gaming industry.

Other benefits include inclusive mentorship, the reality of the level of participants with inclusion of hands-on practical classes and possible career opportunities and job placements.
